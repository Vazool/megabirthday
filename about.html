About Megabirthday.me

Megabirthday is a concept for marking personal growth—not by the orbit of the Earth, but by a more elegant cadence: every 1,000 days.

Why should our personal milestones always fall in the same season, dictated by the same tired loop around the Sun? What if there were a better rhythm—one that makes space for reflection, surprise, and a sense of time as lived?

At Megabirthday, we believe growth is daily, not annual—and that every 1,000 days, you’ve earned the right to celebrate.

A megabirthday marks each 1,000-day milestone since the day you were born. It’s an invitation to pause, take stock, and do something joyful. You're not getting older—you’re getting numerically epic.
